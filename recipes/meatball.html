Homemade Meatballs for Dogs

Description

These meatballs may look mouthwatering, but they are intended for your dog! Best of all, you can use them to get your pup to take medications. Whip some up, sneak in a pill, and you'll be the best dog parent ever.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Eggs or unsweetened applesauce
  • Chopped Parsley
  • Yogurt
  • Almond Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â° Fahrenheit and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silpat mat.
  2. Mix ground beef into a large mixing bowl.
  3. Chop parsley or add dried parsley.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, mixing well.
  5. Form into mini meatballs and place onto the baking sheet.
